About Crikvenica

The Town of Crikvenica is actually a seaside riviera made up of four small coastal towns – Selce, Crikvenica, Dramalj and Jadranovo – connected in a string of seaside resorts. They are located in Kvarner Bay on the coast of the Adriatic Sea. This area is part of the County of Primorje-Gorski Kotar, whose administrative centre is Rijeka, a city located 30 km away. The Town of Crikvenica is not only made up of land. It also stretches into the sea and includes the Vinodol Channel. In total, it covers an area of 60 square kilometres.
Beaches
The first public wooded bathing establishment on the coast in Crikvenica was constructed in 1888. Guests liked it, and returned over and over again, which was the beginning of tourism in the town. All that was needed was better organisation. And that’s exactly what we’ve provided! Our beaches today are very special; the sea leaves nothing to be desired, and nearby there are walking trails and promenades with fragrant Mediterranean plants. It is therefore no wonder that we are described as the riviera with the most beautiful beaches. What is there apart from beaches? Sports and recreational centres Interesting services and facilities for children Excellent food and drinks Entertainment and cultural events for a special atmosphere on summer nights

Apartment

Basic informations

Apartment Lora is a three-room apartment on the ground floor of a family house with a spacious yard located in a quiet part of Crikvenica. Parking for cars is provided in the yard of the apartment. The distance from the city center and the main square and the beach is only 15 minutes' walk.
The apartment is located on an elevation, so you need less time when going to the city and the beach. In the immediate vicinity of the apartment there is also a newly arranged children's playground and guests benefit from complimentary WiFi and private parking available on site.
